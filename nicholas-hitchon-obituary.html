Nicholas Hitchon obituary

June 2024 · 1 minute read

In Madison, Wisconsin, where he was a university professor, Nicholas Hitchon, whose life was followed in the acclaimed British Up documentary films from a precocious age 7 in 1964 to 63 in the 2019 part, passed away from throat cancer. He was 65.

His passing on July 23 was only recently reported on the website of the University of Wisconsin. In the most recent installment of the series, 63 Up, Hitchon revealed his struggle with cancer.

William Nicholas “Nick” Hitchon, who joined the University of Wisconsin-Madison faculty in 1982, was listed as a member of the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department in the university post. He had graduated from Oxford University with a Ph.D. in engineering science the year before.
