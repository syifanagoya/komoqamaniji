Camila Coelho Wiki, Age, Height, Husband, Biography, Family &amp; More

February 2024 · 4 minute read

Camila Coelho (born February 27, 1988) is an American/Brazilian Businesswoman, Influencer, Makeup & Fashion Blogger, YouTube Star, and Instagram Personality from Virginópolis, State of Minas Gerais. She is popular for her two self-entitled YouTube channels.

Furthermore, the 31-year-old Blogger has 3.4+ million subscribers on her main channel. She also has more than 1.2 million subscribers on her second channel where she posts videos in the English language.

Moreover, Camila is famous for her amazing videos like makeup tutorials, hair tutorials, skincare routines, products reviews, Travel Vlogs, and much more. She started her career by posting beauty tips in the Portuguese language on her channel back in June 2010.

In September 2011, the blogger began her English language channel after gaining success. As an Influencer, Coelho also creates fashion-related content on her Instagram account. In addition, she has 7.9+ million followers on the picture sharing platform.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The YouTube star was born on the 27th of February 1988 in Virginópolis, State of Minas Gerais, Brazil. Hence, the present age of Camila Coelho is 31 years old, as of 2019. She raised by her father and mother in Brazil.

But the family moved to the Scranton, Pennsylvania, USA when Camila was very young. Furthermore, she graduated from Scranton High School and completed her school education in 2007.

At the age of 14, the makeup guru moved to Boston where her aunt lived. After completing her graduation, she started to work as a makeup artist in a local makeup store.

Personal Affairs, Spouse & Children

The 31-year-old YouTuber started dating her then-boyfriend, Icaro Brenner in 2005. According to a source, he is also originally from Brazil and grew up in America. Camila tied the knot with her husband on October 16, 2019.

Their wedding ceremony was a private affair and only close friends and family members were invited Furthermore, the couple also shares their pictures of together on their respective Instagram account.

Icaro Brenner also got featured on one of the videos of the Coelho where she asked him about her favorite things. Moreover, they also revealed in the video that the love birds first met on a friend’s birthday party.
