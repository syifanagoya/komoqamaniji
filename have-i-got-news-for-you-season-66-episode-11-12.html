Storyline

The show follows a simple format of four rounds, where the host and the panellists answer questions and make jokes about the news and current affairs of the week. The rounds are:

  • The first round, where the host shows a picture or a video clip to each team and asks them a question related to it.
  • The second round, where the host gives each team a newspaper headline with some words missing and asks them to fill in the blanks.
  • The third round, where the host shows four pictures or names to each team and asks them to identify the odd one out.
  • The fourth round, where the host shows a picture to both teams and asks them to come up with a funny caption for it.

The host also gives bonus points or deducts points for any witty or clever remarks or any incorrect or rude answers. The team with the most points at the end of the show wins.

Ending Explanation

The show ends with the host announcing the final scores and declaring the winning team. The host then thanks the guests and the audience and says goodbye. The credits roll over a montage of funny clips from the episode.
