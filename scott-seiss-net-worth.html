Born in 1994 in Dundalk, Maryland, Scott Seiss‘s journey to fame began in the suburbs of Baltimore.

Seiss’s career took a turn when he started working as a customer service representative at IKEA. This experience, though seemingly mundane, became the bedrock of his viral TikTok character, the Angry Retail Guy.
