In light of Hulk Hogan’s recent divorce with his first wife, it would be nice to take a trip down memory lane.

This article focuses on his children with his first wife, Linda.

Hulk Hogan daughter: Who is Brooke Hogan? Age, husband, children, profession and Instagram

Brooke Hogan, born Brooke Ellen Bollea, is the daughter of WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan and his first wife, reality TV Personality, Linda Hogan.

She is an actress, TV and media personality, singer, model, and former on-screen personality for Impact Wrestling.

She appeared in her family’s reality show, Hogan Knows Best, alongside her family. When personal struggles within her family led to the cancellation of the show, Brooke created her own;  Brooke Knows Best.

Brooke was engaged to former Dallas Cowboys center, Phil Costa, however, the couple were unable to tie the knot.

The engagement ended in November 2013.

Who is his son Nick Hogan?

Nicholas Allan Bollea, formerly known as Nick Hogan is a reality TV personality.

He is best known for appearing alongside his family in their family reality show, Hogan Knows Best. After the show’s cancellation, he also appeared in its spinoff, Brooke Knows Best.
